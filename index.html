Space Station Flight Over the Southern Tip of Italy

The southern tip of Italy is visible in theis image taken by the Expedition 49 crew aboard the International Space Station on Sept. 17, 2016. The brightly lit city of Naples can be seen in the bottom section of the image. A Russian Soyuz spacecraft can be seen in the foreground.
